Chicken Salad

Home

Description

This creamy chicken salad recipe is the best and a family favorite! Everyone needs this best chicken salad recipe in their collection!

This creamy homemade chicken salad is quick and easy to make. With just a handful of everyday ingredients to make - it's perfect for warm weather picnics ,lunches or light dinners.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds

  • ½ cup mayonnaise

  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat

  • 1 rib celery, chopped

Steps

  1. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.

  2. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl.

  3. Toss the chicken, toasted almonds, celery and your mixed mayonnaise together.

  4. Enjoy your quick and easy salad!
